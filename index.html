About the Chapter

A student-run organization preparing River Valley Pirates for careers in business, finance, and leadership.

FBLA RVHS was founded in 2023 to serve commerce and leadership‑leaning students of River Valley High School. Since then, the chapter has helped more than 500 students familiarize themselves with the perceived hard concepts of finance, leadership, and business.

The chapter has also sent a significant number of delegates to the State Leadership Conference every year since 2024, competing across events from accounting to sports & entertainment management.
