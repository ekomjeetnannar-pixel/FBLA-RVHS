Why Join FBLA

Four reasons Falcon students walk away glad they joined.

Skill Building
Learn real business, finance, and leadership skills through competitive events and hands-on projects.
Conferences
Compete at Regional, Section, and State Leadership Conferences alongside chapters from across California.
Leadership
Run for chapter office, plan events, and build the kind of leadership experience colleges and employers notice.
Community
Join a chapter of Falcon students who support each other through every competition season.
FAQ

Who can join?

Any River Valley High School student interested in business, finance, or leadership — no prior experience required.

Do I need to compete to be a member?

No — competing at conferences is optional. Many members join for the leadership and community-service side of the chapter.

How do I sign up?

Reach out to Mr. Talstyka in Room 811, or email dtalstyka@ycusd.org for current meeting times and next steps.

What happens if I place at the State Leadership Conference?

Top state qualifiers in each competitive event earn the right to represent California at FBLA's National Leadership Conference, held in a different city each summer.

Does FBLA look good on college applications?

Yes — officer roles, competitive event placements, and community service hours through FBLA are all concrete, resume-ready leadership experience that colleges and employers recognize.

Ready to lead with us?

For any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to our advisor, Mr. Talstyka, in Room 811.
