About the Chapter

The story of FBLA RVHS, from our founding to the guests, competitions, and community that shape us.

FBLA RVHS was founded in 2023 to serve commerce and leadership‑leaning students of River Valley High School. Since then, the chapter has helped more than 500 students familiarize themselves with the perceived hard concepts of finance, leadership, and business.

The chapter has also sent a significant number of delegates to the State Leadership Conference every year since 2024, competing across events from accounting to sports & entertainment management.

RVHS FBLA is a local chapter of Future Business Leaders of America, a national nonprofit career and technical student organization founded in 1940. FBLA prepares middle school, high school, and college students for careers in business and business‑related fields through competitive events, leadership conferences, and community service — with hundreds of thousands of members across chartered chapters nationwide.
